ShopEase - Website

ShopEase is an e-commerce website developed as a mini-project during my studies at LBS College of Engineering, Kasaragod. The project aimed to create a user-friendly and efficient platform for online shopping, integrating advanced features and technologies to enhance the user experience.

Website to Analyze Defects in Railway Tracks (May 2024)

Railway Track Defect Analyzer

I developed a website to analyze defects in railway tracks and suggest the solution to take in future. By aggregating data from visual inspections and ultrasonic testing, the website provides a comprehensive overview of track conditions. It features dynamic data visualization, allowing railway maintenance personnel to easily navigate and access relevant information.

Poll Website Project - Hexmos (January 2022 to March 2022)

Pollify: Create, Filter & Vote on Dynamic Polls

Collaborating with the HEXMOS team, we developed Pollify, a feature-rich fullstack web application. Built using Django, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Ajax, and jQuery, Pollify empowers users to create polls, view them on the home page, filter polls by tags, explore detailed poll displays, and cast their votes. Our dedicated team, consisting of Lince Mathew, Shrijith Venkatramana, Varun Deshpande, and Athreya, worked diligently to bring this interactive polling platform to life.
